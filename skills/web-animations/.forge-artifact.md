Tier 3 zero-JS (3 NEW):
- `CSSScrollReveal` — `animation-timeline: view()`, falls back to opacity:1 on unsupported browsers or `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`. # source: https://developer.chrome.com/articles/scroll-driven-animations/
- `CSSScrollProgress` — `animation-timeline: scroll()`, fixed-top reading progress bar with `scaleX`. # source: same article
- `TransitionLink` + `startTransition()` — `document.startViewTransition()` wrapper, auto-bypass on `e.hasUAVisualTransition`. # source: https://developer.chrome.com/docs/web-platform/view-transitions/same-document

Every kit component carries a `// web-animations: Tier N (Tn.m Name)` marker comment.
