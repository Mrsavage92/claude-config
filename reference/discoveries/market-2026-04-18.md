**This is what market scan looks like when you don't have a clear unfair advantage.** The strongest SaaS builds come from:
- Distribution you already own (AuditHQ customers, MuleSoft clients)
- Domain expertise incumbents lack (AU-specific compliance, vertical depth)

`market` mode is blind to these — it surfaces broad gaps, not Adam-specific advantages.
