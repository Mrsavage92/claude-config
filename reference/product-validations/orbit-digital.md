### 3. Market gap — **PASS**

Three specific gaps:

- **Multi-suite (9 categories) at single price point.** All paid AU competitors specialise (SEO-only, reputation-only, listings-only). None bundle technical + marketing + GEO + security + privacy + social + reputation + employer brand + AI readiness in one diagnostic.
- **Rescan proof methodology.** Most providers say "fixed". Almost none rescan to prove before/after delta. The deterministic engine makes this defensible.
- **AU-first regulatory framing.** Spam Act 2003 (SMS sender ID), AU Privacy Act 1988 (consent + data handling), AU local SEO patterns. US/global incumbents (Birdeye, Podium) don't tune to AU compliance specifically.

### 6. Moat — **PASS (strong)**
Multiple compounding advantages:

- **Proprietary data engine.** AuditHQ's 500+ deterministic checks across 9 suites is owner-built. Not licensable. Competitors would need 6–12 months to build comparable depth.
- **Domain expertise.** Adam built AuditHQ end-to-end — knows the 9 suites in depth, edge cases, AU regulatory specifics.
- **Cost advantage.** AuditHQ engine runs at near-zero marginal cost per audit (Anthropic + Supabase combined ~$10/audit). Competitors using human-time-heavy audit methodology can't match unit economics.
- **Timing — AI/GEO readiness.** Most AU agencies don't yet audit for AI search citability (AI Overviews, ChatGPT citations, Perplexity grounding). Orbit Digital ships this from day 1 as part of the 9-suite cross-section.
- **Rescan methodology.** Most agencies don't have the infrastructure to consistently rescan + before/after delta. Engine-backed methodology makes this trivial.

⚠️ **Weak spot:** distribution. Adam has zero warm audience in salons/clinics/tradies. Engine + expertise are real, distribution is not. Mitigated by: AU local Facebook group seeding, podcast outreach, agency partner referrals — none yet executed.

## VALIDATE-FIRST → Interview Protocol

**Talk to 5 AU SMB owners in target verticals before any further marketing spend.**

Target list (specific):
- 2× salon/beauty owners (Sunshine Coast, Brisbane)
- 1× allied health clinic (physio, chiro, podiatry — Brisbane or Gold Coast)
- 1× tradie (electrician or plumber — single-owner with 1–5 employees)
- 1× wedding supplier OR tourism/accommodation operator

**Script:**
1. "How do you currently know if your website is actually working for you?" (open-ended pain)
2. "What's the most painful or invisible part of your digital setup — the thing you can't see but probably matters?"
3. "What would you pay, one-off, to get a clear report telling you exactly what to fix and what order to fix it in?"
4. "If I described that as a $1,950 audit with a 30-page report, rescore after fixes, and 6-week follow-up — would you pay $1,950 for it? Yes/no/conditions."
5. "Can I show you a sample report in 2 weeks?"

**Return condition:** ≥3 named "yes I'd pay $1,950 for that" responses + contact info.
→ Re-run validator with buyer evidence attached → BUILD verdict unblocked → scale marketing.

**If <3 yes answers:** the gap is awareness or wrong vertical, not market. Test:
- Different vertical (e.g. allied health vs salons)
- Different framing (e.g. "lead leak audit" vs "digital ecosystem audit")
- Lower entry price (e.g. $497 "preview audit" upsold to $1,950)
